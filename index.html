From Offering to Essence

Our controlled, ethical collection and extraction workflow preserves aromatic integrity while ensuring full traceability and regulatory compliance.

1 · Collection

Partnered temples & churches. Clean, segregated floral feedstock under MoUs.

2 · Pre‑processing

Sorting, drying, and cold storage to retain volatile compounds.

3 · Extraction

Steam / hydro distillation & solvent‑free tech. Batch CoAs issued.

4 · QA & Fulfilment

GC‑MS, microbial checks, allergen sheets. Food‑grade drums & glass.
